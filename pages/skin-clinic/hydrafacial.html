What is a Hydrafacial?

A Hydrafacial is a medical-grade resurfacing treatment that clears out pores and hydrates the skin using a patented vortex-fusion delivery system. It is suitable for all skin types and delivers immediate results with no downtime.

Unlike traditional facials, the Hydrafacial uses a unique spiral suction tip to simultaneously exfoliate and extract impurities while bathing the skin in nourishing serums.

  • Instantly brighter, smoother skin
  • Reduced fine lines and wrinkles
  • Cleared congestion and blackheads

The Vortex-Fusion Technology

The Hydrafacial works in three distinct steps. First, Cleanse & Peel removes dead skin cells to reveal fresh skin. Second, Extract & Hydrate uses vortex suction to remove debris from pores while delivering intense moisture. Finally, Fuse & Protect saturates the skin's surface with antioxidants and peptides.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any downtime?

No. You can apply makeup and return to normal activities immediately.

How often should I get one?

For maintenance, every 4-6 weeks is ideal. For correcting specific concerns, a course of 3 monthly treatments is recommended.

Does it hurt?

Not at all. Most clients describe it as a refreshing, tingling sensation.
