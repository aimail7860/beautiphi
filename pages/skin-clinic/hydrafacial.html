What is Hydrafacial?

Hydrafacial is a medical-grade hydradermabrasion device that performs three essential steps in one treatment: Cleanse, Extract, and Hydrate.

Unlike traditional microdermabrasion, Hydrafacial uses a patented vortex-fusion delivery system to remove dead skin cells while simultaneously bathing the skin with cleansing, exfoliating, and hydrating solutions.

  • ✅ Instant Results: See a glow immediately.
  • ✅ No Downtime: Return to work immediately.
  • ✅ All Skin Types: Safe for sensitive skin.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the treatment take?

A typical Hydrafacial treatment takes approximately 30 minutes. Many clients fit it into their lunch break.

Is there any downtime?

No. You may experience slight redness immediately after, but it subsides quickly. You can apply makeup and return to normal activities immediately.

How often should I get a Hydrafacial?

For best results, we recommend a series of treatments every 4 weeks. For maintenance, once every 3 months is sufficient.
